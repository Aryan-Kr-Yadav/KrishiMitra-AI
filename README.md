# 🌾 KrishiMitra AI
### Empowering Farmers with Intelligent Crop Assistance

KrishiMitra AI is an AI-powered agricultural assistant designed to help farmers identify crop diseases, receive treatment recommendations, and discover relevant government schemes. The platform aims to make expert agricultural guidance more accessible through an intuitive and multilingual interface.

Built for the **Agents for Good** track, KrishiMitra AI combines the power of modern web technologies and Generative AI to support farmers in making informed decisions and reducing crop losses.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🔍 AI Crop Disease Diagnosis
- Upload images of affected crops for disease analysis.
- Diagnose diseases based on symptom descriptions.
- Supports multiple crop types.

### 💊 Treatment Recommendations
- Organic treatment suggestions.
- Chemical treatment recommendations.
- Preventive measures to avoid future outbreaks.
- Immediate action steps for farmers.

### 🏛 Government Scheme Assistant
- Discover relevant agricultural schemes based on crop and state.
- View eligibility criteria and required documents.
- Access official resources and application guidance.

### 🌐 Multilingual Support
- English support.
- Hindi support.
- Language toggle for improved accessibility.

### 📱 Responsive Experience
- Mobile-first design.
- Clean and intuitive user interface.
- Accessible and farmer-friendly workflows.

## 🧠 How It Works

### Image-Based Diagnosis

1. Farmer uploads an image of the affected crop.
2. The backend processes the image.
3. Gemini Vision analyzes the crop condition.
4. The AI generates:
   - Disease prediction
   - Severity level
   - Treatment recommendations
   - Preventive measures
5. Results are displayed in the dashboard.

---

### Symptom-Based Diagnosis

1. Farmer enters crop symptoms.
2. Gemini analyzes the provided description.
3. The system identifies the likely disease.
4. Recommendations are generated instantly.

---

### Government Scheme Assistance

1. Farmer selects:
   - State
   - Crop
   - Preferred language
2. Gemini retrieves relevant agricultural schemes.
3. Details such as eligibility and benefits are presented.

## 🤖 AI Prompt Strategy

KrishiMitra AI leverages structured prompting to ensure reliable responses.

The AI provides:

- Disease identification
- Confidence estimation
- Severity classification
- Organic remedies
- Chemical remedies
- Preventive actions
- Expert consultation guidance

The system clearly communicates uncertainty whenever confidence is low.
